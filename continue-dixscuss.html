Welcome Dixcuss - A Safe and Open Space

Dixcuss is designed to be a safe and open space where you can freely express your thoughts, feelings, and experiences without fear of judgment. Whether you're seeking a listening ear, sharing your struggles, or simply looking for support, this platform allows you to connect anonymously.
Dixcuss is a dynamic social platform under Soulaze, a leading counseling hub dedicated to mental wellness and emotional support.

Key Features of Dixcuss

✓ Anonymous Conversations: You can comment as a guest without creating a profile. Even if you input your email, it will not be displayed or reflected on Dixcuss. The only thing that will be visible is your nickname. You are advised to use "Anonymous" as your nickname if you want to remain completely anonymous without signing up. This allows you to share your thoughts freely and participate in discussions anonymously.


✓ Dix – The Unique Way to Comment: On Dixcuss, comments are called Dix. For example, you can say, "Check out my Dix on Dixcuss!"
